Our Best Chef

Chef Ana Klevin

Chef Ana Klevin is a passionate culinary artist with a deep love for traditional sweets and an innovative flair for reinventing them. With over 15 years of experience in the kitchen, Anaya has honed her skills in crafting exquisite mithai that marries time-honored recipes with contemporary twists. Born and raised in a family of food enthusiasts, Anaya's journey into the world of sweets began at a young age, where she spent countless hours in her grandmother's kitchen learning the secrets of authentic Indian desserts.

Chef Riya Mehta

Chef Riya Mehta is a visionary in the world of desserts, known for her ability to blend tradition with innovation. With a career spanning over a decade, Riya has established herself as a creative force in the culinary arts, particularly in the realm of mithai. Growing up in a bustling Indian household, Riya's earliest memories revolve around the aroma of freshly made sweets during festivals and family gatherings. Inspired by her mother's and grandmother's love for cooking, Riya pursued her passion by studying at a top culinary school, where she mastered both traditional and modern techniques.
